musical

I've been a musician for most of my life, and I have been a singer-songwriter since I was 16 and I stole my oldest brother's guitar.

In college, I went to every single open mic night that was available to me. I had a couple of solo concerts. I performed at campus events, including new student orientation, fundraisers, and the Arts Walk. It was possibly the best part of my college experience.

Now, most of my performing is online. I post new music over at YouTube as Lenny Wonders.

In February 2024, I participated in the February Album Writing Month challenge and successfully wrote 14 songs that month, almost all of which I am quite proud of. The FAWM playlist on YouTube has all of those songs, starting with my least favorite (I can't resist starting a playlist with "the end;" sometimes I make silly decisions!)
